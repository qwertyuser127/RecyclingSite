About Smart Recycling

Why we built this

Smart Recycling aims to fix everyday recycling problems in schools by making sorting easier, clearer, and more engaging.

Based on real contamination and sorting issues observed in schools.

See the main problem View all 15 issues

What Smart Recycling is

A student-built site combining an AI recycling assistant, a mini-game, an interactive story, and a quiz — all designed to make good sorting habits stick.

Who it's for

Students and staff who want a clearer, more engaging way to understand what goes where — and why it matters.

What's next

We're expanding with QR-coded bins, real-time contamination stats, and more story chapters from Recylo.
